# Instructions for Privacy, Terms, and Data Deletion Generation

## App Context
- **Name**: PlateWatchers
- **Business Model**: A community-driven restaurant leaderboard/ranking app.
- **Data Collection**: We collect the user's Facebook Name, Email, and Profile Picture via Facebook Login.
- **Data Usage**: The name and picture are used to display who cast votes on the leaderboard. The email is used as a unique identifier to prevent duplicate voting.
